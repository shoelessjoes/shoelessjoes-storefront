## Store Overview (short — for meta / homepage intro)

Shoeless Joe's in Cincinnati, OH is your one-stop Sports Card & Collectibles store. We offer supplies, single sports cards (raw & graded), sealed packs & boxes, Pokémon & more. With years of customer-service experience and a passion for the hobby, we aim to provide knowledge and education about all your sports card and memorabilia needs. Starting young collectors out in the hobby is a focal point of our vision as a community card shop.

## About Us (long — for About page)

Shoeless Joe's Cards is proudly operated by brothers Kevin and Dan Burke, lifelong Cincinnati residents and passionate collectors. We've been visiting Shoeless Joe's since it first opened its doors and have deep roots in the local hobby community.

In 2022, during the hobby's post-COVID resurgence, we launched our own card business focused on grading, collecting, and resale. Our experience includes 1,000+ card submissions to PSA, BGS, SGC, and other grading companies. We've been active on eBay since 2001 and continue to engage with PWCC, Goldin, Collectors, Fanatics, and Rally Rd.

Our goal is to blend the classic card-shop feel of Shoeless Joe's with modern tools and services that meet the needs of today's collectors.

## History — "Then & Now" (4 eras, photo-driven)

**1. The Beginning (Mid-1990s) — Cards, Creamy Whip & Community**
Before it became a Cincinnati hobby staple, Shoeless Joe's started as a baseball card shop and neighborhood creamy whip — serving slushies, nachos, and summertime memories. The early signage is something many locals still remember.

**2. The Tom Era (1997–2025) — Tom Huber's Legacy**
In the late '90s, Tom Huber took over and spent nearly three decades building one of the most diverse card and collectible inventories in the region. His love for the hobby turned the shop into a time capsule. Tom's presence, generosity, and passion made it more than a store — it was a part of the community's fabric.

**3. The Storm & Restoration (April 2025) — A Rocky Start to a New Chapter**
Just one week after new ownership took the reins, a major storm tore through the region, damaging the roof and causing significant flooding. The floors, walls, and ceiling had to be replaced — but we worked hard to keep the heart and soul of Shoeless Joe's intact.

**4. The Burke Family Era (2025–Present) — Continuing the Story**
Now operated by Dan and Kevin Burke, second-generation collectors, Shoeless Joe's enters a new chapter. Same local roots. Same collector spirit. A fresh look, ready for the next generation.
